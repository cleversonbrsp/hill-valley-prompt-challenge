Action:

2) Identify prioritized savings opportunities to hit Goldie's goal: **15% reduction next quarter without degrading SLA**.
3) For each opportunity, include:
   - a short title
   - estimated monthly savings range (USD) grounded in the line items (use reasonable engineering assumptions; label assumptions explicitly)
   - **percent of total monthly spend** represented by that opportunity (use your computed total as denominator)
   - implementation effort: Low / Medium / High
   - risks / prerequisites / mitigations (especially anything that could affect availability or latency)
4) Sort opportunities by **impact** (largest expected % of total first).
5) End with a short "gap-to-goal" section: whether the portfolio covers >=15%, and what to do if not.

Goal:
Produce a concise report Goldie can read in under 5 minutes, with executive tone, actionable sequencing, and explicit alignment to the 15% target.
